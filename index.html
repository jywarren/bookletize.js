Bookletize.js

Reformat PDFs into booklets in 4,1,2,3 or 8,1,2,7/6,3,4,5 format in JavaScript for making bound paper booklets. Print the resulting PDF, fold in half and staple to form a booklet.
Open Source by Jeffrey Yoo Warren
